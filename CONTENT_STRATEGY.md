## Content Automation Strategy

### Daily Workflow
1. **Morning (6AM):** Scrape tech news from sources
2. **Midday (12PM):** Generate 3-5 articles
3. **Evening (6PM):** Publish and schedule social posts

### Content Sources
- TechCrunch AI section
- Reuters Technology
- The Verge
- Ars Technica
- HackerNews trending
- Company press releases

### Article Templates
1. **Breaking News:** 300-400 words, fast turnaround
2. **Deep Dive:** 800-1200 words, analysis + context
3. **Review:** 1000-1500 words, hands-on testing
4. **Roundup:** 600-800 words, list format

### SEO Optimization
- Keywords in headlines
- Meta descriptions
- Structured data (JSON-LD)
- Internal linking
- Image alt tags
